She looks but she doesn’t see
I wish but I cannot seem to be
the focus of her sight
someone help me.

I’d help myself
but I’m not free
I’m locked in a world
that stays goofy.

I’m a prisoner
not a trophy.

If she would open
the doors to eternity
my heart would be hers
eternally.
